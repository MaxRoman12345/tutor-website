A structured path to exam confidence

A programme that meets students where they are.

Every student starts with a clear picture of their current level. From there, lessons, homework and practice are shaped around exactly what will move them forward.

Clear next steps, every week.

The programme

01Baseline assessment

A diagnostic test shows the tutor exactly where to begin and which gaps need attention first.

02Targeted topic coverage

Lessons and worksheets then build secure understanding across every topic, at the right pace.

03Old-spec mixed papers

Mixed old-spec questions strengthen fluency and help students apply knowledge beyond single topics.

04Timed new-spec papers

Finally, new-spec papers are completed to time under exam conditions to build real confidence.

Week by week

What students can expect each week.

Each week follows the same simple rhythm: a focused lesson, relevant work to complete at home, and a chance to resolve any sticking points together in the next session.

01

A focused lesson

Your tutor targets the topics that will make the biggest difference, using your progress and recent work.

02

Around two hours of homework

Relevant questions are set in the software after each lesson, with clear work to complete before you meet again.

03

Self-mark and review

Mark the questions yourself, then bring the difficult ones to the next lesson so your tutor can help you move on.
